Blockchain Innovation Week

This week will be a special one! Two full days where experts share the newest developments and insights in blockchain technologies.

Blockchain Innovation Week presents a program of lectures, workshops, interactive panel discussions and time for connecting with others in this emerging field. Are you a developer looking for hands-on practice? Or a business expert who wants to connect with innovative blockchain startups and ready to be inspired by visions of the future?
